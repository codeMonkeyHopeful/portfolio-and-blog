Color Strategy for Senior Engineering Managers

  • Dark backgrounds: Show technical depth and modern sensibilities
  • Bright accent colors: Demonstrate innovation and forward-thinking
  • High contrast: Ensures accessibility and professional readability
  • Consistent system: Use 60% dark, 30% neutral, 10% accent for balance
  • Industry alignment: Blues for fintech, greens for health/sustainability, purples for AI/ML
  • Personal brand: Choose colors that feel authentic to your leadership style
  • Stand out subtly: Be memorable without being flashy - you want to be taken seriously
